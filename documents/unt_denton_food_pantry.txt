Food Pantry Hours

Food Pantry Hours, Guidelines, and Community Resources

To serve as many students as we can, students are only able to use the pantry once per week. New student patrons must bring their student ID the first time they visit. Returning patrons must bring their student ID, food pantry card, and grocery bag. 
Summer 2026 Hours
May 11, 2026 - August 15, 2026
Monday - Wednesday: 9 a.m. - 5 p.m.
Thursday: Closed
Friday: 9 a.m. - 5 p.m.
Saturday - Sunday: Closed

The Pantry will be closed Monday, May 25 (Memorial Day); Friday, June 19 (Juneteenth); and F-Sa, July 3-4 (Fourth of July).

Mobile Pantries do not occur over the summer; the next Mobile Pantry will be in Septemeber 2026.

If any of our open times do not work for you, please email us at DESresources@unt.edu and we can schedule an alternative visit time.

Otherwise, we follow the university closure calendar and are closed whenever the university is closed.
Additional Resources

Below are a few more food resources in Denton. You can find additional resources in the DFW area on our off campus resource page. There is also a small food pantry at UNT Frisco, which can be accessed by visiting their information desk.

We understand that access to healthy meals is important for student success and well-being. In addition to campus resources, there are several food pantries in the Denton area that can help provide groceries and essentials when you need them. These community resources are here to support you and your family, making it easier to stay nourished and focused on your goals.
